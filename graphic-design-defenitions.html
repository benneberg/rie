01. Foundation

Design Principles

01

Brutalist Precision

Raw, unpolished aesthetics combined with Swiss-style grid systems. Exposed structure as design feature. No decorative elements without function.

02

Kinetic Data

Information in motion. Animated typography, living charts, and micro-interactions that guide the eye without overwhelming.

03

Asymmetric Balance

Editorial layouts that break the grid intentionally. Visual tension through misalignment creates dynamic reading experiences.
